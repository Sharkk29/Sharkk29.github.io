Mili



Home | Sinners | Project Moon

Mili is an international indie band. They have a history with Project Moon, creating seven songs in their second work "Librairy of Ruina". In Limbus Company, they are responsible for creating the boss music in the end of each canto as well as the game's main theme song. They are largely praised and loved by the community, with each song further enhancing the experience of the cantos. Below is the list of their works in Limbus company specifically.

  • "In Hell We Live, Lament" feat. KIHOW from MYTH&ROID

    The Main Theme Song of Limbus Company.

  • "Between Two Worlds"

    Canto 3's battle theme.

  • "Fly, My Wings"

    Canto 4's battle theme.

  • "Compass"

    Canto 5's battle theme.

  • "Through Patches of Violet"

    Canto 6's battle theme.

  • "Hero"

    Canto 7's battle theme.

  • "TIAN TIAN"

    Canto 8's battle theme.
