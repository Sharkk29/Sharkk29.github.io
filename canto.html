Cantos are the main story chatpers of the game. They further push the narrative while also providing unique focus to one of it's sinners. We often see the sinner's backstory, their greatest villains or enemies, understand their traumas and push them towards confronting their past. While we many not always succeed, each step allows each sinner to continue to grow.


  1. Gregor's Canto and the tutorial. We learn of his time during the war, the transformation of his arm and the difficulties he faced beacuse of it. A disgraced war vertan who couldn't find peace as he ran away.

  2. Rodion's Canto and the end of the tutorial. We learn of her poverty ridden past, her revolt against the upper class, and the consequences of her impulsive actions.

  3. Sinclair's Canto. We learn of his school, his family, and his entire town become ruined due to a fanatical cult which hates any aesthetic used to replace the human body. His sorrow, rage, and inaction are tested all at once.

  4. Yi Sang's Canto. A once inspired scientist, looking to breakthrough with colleagues now carries the burden of losing everyone dear to him. He finds within him the ideal to move forward, and set his wings free.
